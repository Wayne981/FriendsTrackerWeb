Our mission

Friends Energy Tracker was born from a simple observation: not all social interactions leave us feeling the same way. Some friends energize and inspire us, while others might unintentionally drain our emotional resources.

We believe that awareness is the first step toward building healthier, more fulfilling relationships. By tracking how different social interactions affect your energy levels, you can make more intentional choices about how you spend your social time.

How it works

Our approach is refreshingly simple

  • After spending time with friends, rate your energy level on a scale of 1-5
  • Track patterns over time to identify energy-boosting and energy-draining relationships
  • Receive personalized insights about your social patterns
  • Make informed decisions about your social calendar

The app uses your data to provide gentle guidance , helping you recognize which relationships deserve more of your time and which might benefit from boundaries.
